Who We Are

Not a training centre.
An ecosystem.

"We didn't build Pheel to give people fish. We built it so they could own the whole fishing industry."

Kenya has no shortage of talent. What it has a shortage of is structure — the kind that turns raw skill into real income, scattered hustle into sustainable business, and isolated creatives into a powerful community.

Pheel Creations Hub was built to be that structure. We operate as a hybrid platform that combines training, incubation, and ecosystem development to support emerging creatives and entrepreneurs in building sustainable livelihoods — with impact that reaches across Kenya.

We run on revenue, not goodwill. That's by design. Because when your model is sustainable, your impact is permanent.

Our Story

Built to bridge the gap between talent and income.


Pheel Creations Hub is a creative and innovation hub focused on empowering youth — with a strong emphasis on women — through skills development, business support, and access to economic opportunities.

The hub operates as a hybrid platform that combines training, incubation, and ecosystem development to support emerging creatives and entrepreneurs in building sustainable livelihoods.

Pheel Creations Hub is designed to bridge the gap between talent and income by equipping individuals with skills, supporting business growth, and connecting them to real opportunities — including markets, partnerships, and funding.

A significant portion of our participants are women, reflecting the hub's strong commitment to women's economic empowerment. This isn't a tagline. It's our daily reality, built into our programs, our schedules, and our pricing.

Our Vision

"To build a leading creative and innovation hub that equips, incubates, and connects creatives and entrepreneurs — especially women — to sustainable income and growth opportunities."
